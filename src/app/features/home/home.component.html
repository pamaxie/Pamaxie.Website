safe. space.

Our solution offers multiple things to create a safe spaces for your users. One includes the detection of symbols of hate speech to limit them to private spaces. Others are detection for common types of unwanted content like sexual interactions, nudity and gore. And we are working on developing “trigger filters” that detect certain types of phobia content so you can warn your users about them.

user. privacy.

our solution was developed to offer the best data privacy possible. This means we do not collect or store any data that isn’t necessary for us.
We also openly talk about how we use every piece of user data that we get and what we do with it of if we store it.
We believe security does not have to mean omitting user privacy or risking having their data sold to advertisers along the process.
