our. mission.

We believe that content scanning and filtering is important to anyone in this day an age. There is a lot of bad actors online and sadly they won’t go away easily. Sadly there isn’t a lot of good open source solutions and that’s why we were created. Tons of anti-virus software is closed source or does not publicize nearly enough research / information papers. Similar things go for image detection systems developed for content moderation.
We believe this is an issue that affects all of us and we should work together to build a safer, better internet in these regards.
